My case studies


How do people prepare their grocery shopping?

A comprehensive generative research study conducted in 2024/25 as a volunteer UX Researcher to understand user behaviours, needs and pain points in grocery shopping preparation. The research explored how people decide where to shop, manage budgets and leverage promotions. Findings informed the development of a price comparison tool that helps users save money by tracking product prices across different supermarkets.

Keywords: UX Research, Generative Research, Price Comparison, Grocery Shopping, Consumer Behaviour, Task Analysis, User Knowledge Profiles, Shopping Preparation, Inflation Response, Money-Saving Tools, Shopping Lists, Competitive Analysis, Budget Management, Supermarket Selection, User Interviews
