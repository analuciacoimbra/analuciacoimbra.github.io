My case studies


How do people prepare their grocery shopping?

A comprehensive generative research study conducted in 2024/25 as a volunteer UX Researcher to understand user behaviours, needs and pain points in grocery shopping preparation. The research explored how people decide where to shop, manage budgets and leverage promotions. Findings reframed the product strategy and informed the development roadmap for a tool designed to help users save money during grocery shopping.

Keywords: UX Research, Generative Research, Price Comparison, Grocery Shopping, Consumer Behaviour, Task Analysis, User Knowledge Profiles, Shopping Preparation, Inflation Response, Money-Saving Tools, Shopping Lists, Competitive Analysis, Budget Management, Supermarket Selection, User Interviews

How do therapists manage their daily practice?

A generative study I carried out during my participation in the "Empreende Já" initiative (an entrepreneurship programme) in 2017, which allowed me to better understand the pains and needs of therapists. This study was used as the basis for the business plan presented at the end of the programme under the project name "Inspiring Care".
