How do people prepare their grocery shopping?

A comprehensive generative research study conducted in 2024/25 as a UX Researcher to understand user behaviours, needs and pain points in grocery shopping preparation. The research explored how people decide where to shop, manage budgets and leverage promotions. Findings informed the development of a price comparison tool that helps users save money by tracking product prices across different supermarkets.
